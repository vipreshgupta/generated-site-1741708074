🔥 Key Features & Strategy Breakdown

✅ Long Entry Conditions:

  • Breakout Strategy: The price must break above the highest high of the last 20 days (Upper Channel).
  • Momentum Confirmation: A "Strong Buy" signal is confirmed when:
    • RSI > 55
    • MACD > 0
    • ADX > 30
  • Alternative Entry: Positive price momentum where the rate of increase is accelerating.
  • Hawkes Process Validation: Ensures optimal trade execution timing.
  • Levy Process Requirement: Introduced as a mandatory condition for improving profitability and Sharpe ratio, though it reduces trade frequency.

❌ Short Entry Conditions:

  • Breakdown Strategy: The price must break below the lowest low of the last 20 days (Lower Channel).
  • Momentum Confirmation: A "Strong Sell" signal is confirmed when:
    • RSI < 45
    • MACD < 0
    • ADX > 30
  • Alternative Entry: Negative price momentum where the decline is accelerating.
  • Hawkes Process Validation: Ensures optimal entry.
  • Levy Process Requirement: Used as a mandatory condition, enhancing profitability and reducing unnecessary trades.
